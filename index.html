Project Objective

Install permanent steel bollards to protect the exterior of the building from vehicle impact. The process includes cutting asphalt, setting structural pipes in deep concrete footings, and filling cores with high-strength concrete for maximum long-term durability.

Important Planning & Safety Notes

CRITICAL NOTE: Underground Utilities

Before any digging begins, we must contact the local utility marking service (e.g., 811 or "Call Before You Dig"). Even though we are in a parking lot, gas lines, electrical conduits for parking lights, and water mains can run directly under the asphalt. This call must be made at least 48:72 hours before work starts.

Safety Standards (DC-82-E)

Job Hazard Analysis (DC-85)

A JHA must be performed before installation to address risks such as flying debris from the jackhammer and heavy lifting of concrete bags.
